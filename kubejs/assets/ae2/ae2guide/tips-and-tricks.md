# Tips and Tricks

A load of random little recommendations

## Structures Generation

The structure generation has been tweaked. The structures are staged behind corresponding stages. Until you unlock the specific stage, the structures will not generate. Try to generate the smallest amount of chuncks at the early stage of the ages. PreGeneration mods will break the advancement locking yourself forever and prevent you to finish the modpack.
